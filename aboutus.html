History of SDA :

Seventh Day Adventist Matric Higher Secondary School, Coimbatore was established in 1975 under the guidance of the Seventh-day Adventist educational management, with a noble vision of providing quality education rooted in strong moral and spiritual values. From its humble beginnings, the school has grown into a respected institution known for academic excellence, discipline, and character formation. Over the decades, the school has consistently focused on holistic education, nurturing students intellectually, morally, socially, and spiritually. With a dedicated team of educators and the continued support of parents and the community, SDA School remains committed to shaping responsible, confident, and value-driven individuals who contribute positively to society.

Mr. Joe Thomas

Principal of Seventh Day Adventist Matric Higher Secondary School, Coimbatore

is a dedicated educational leader committed to academic excellence, discipline, and value-based education. With a strong belief in holistic development, he encourages students to grow intellectually, morally, and socially. Under his guidance, the school strives to create a nurturing learning environment that inspires confidence, responsibility, and lifelong learning among students.

Vice Principal

The Vice Principal of Seventh Day Adventist Matric Higher Secondary School, Coimbatore plays a vital role in supporting the academic and administrative leadership of the institution. With a strong commitment to discipline, academic excellence, and student welfare, the Vice Principal works closely with the Principal, teachers, and parents to ensure a positive and effective learning environment. By encouraging teamwork, integrity, and holistic development, the Vice Principal helps guide students to grow into responsible, confident, and value-oriented individuals.
