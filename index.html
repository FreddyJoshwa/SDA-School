Motto & Aim

Motto 👁️

“The Love of Christ Constraineth Us” is the foundation of our faith and values. The love of Christ guides our thoughts, words, and actions every day. It teaches us to live with purity, discipline, and kindness. We are inspired to serve others selflessly and compassionately. Christ’s love encourages obedience to God and respect for all. We strive to reflect His character in every aspect of our lives.

Aim 🎯

“The Adventist message to all the world in this generation” is our mission. We seek to share God’s love, hope, and message of salvation. Our aim is to nurture young minds with strong moral and spiritual values. We guide students to live according to biblical principles. We encourage faith, responsibility, and service to humanity. Our goal is to prepare students to be a blessing to society and faithful servants of God..

Facilities

Our school offers well-equipped classrooms, laboratories, sports and activity spaces to support holistic student development.

Biology Laboratory

A fully equipped lab with microscopes and specimens to help students explore and understand living organisms.

Physics Laboratory

A well-equipped lab that helps students understand physical concepts through practical experiments and hands-on learning.

Chemistry Laboratory

A modern laboratory designed for safe and effective chemical experiments, encouraging scientific thinking and curiosity.

Library

A quiet and resourceful space with a wide collection of books to promote reading, research, and learning.

Computer Lab

A modern computer lab with updated systems to develop digital skills and support technology-based learning.
